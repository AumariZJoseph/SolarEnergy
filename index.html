Solar Energy Tips

Optimal Panel Placement

Position solar panels facing south for maximum sun exposure throughout the day. Clean panels regularly to maintain efficiency.

Water Heater Maintenance

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do solar water heaters last?

Solar water heaters typically last 15-20 years with proper maintenance. Our Mega Sun systems come with a 3-year warranty and are designed for durability in Caribbean climates.

Do solar systems work on cloudy days?

Yes! Our evacuated tube technology is designed to capture solar energy even on cloudy days. The vacuum insulation helps maintain heat efficiency in various weather conditions.

What maintenance is required?

Regular cleaning of tubes, annual system inspection, and checking for leaks. We offer comprehensive maintenance services to keep your system running efficiently.

How much can I save with solar?

Solar water heaters can reduce your electricity bill by 50-80% for water heating. The exact savings depend on your usage patterns and local energy costs.

Is installation complicated?

No, our experienced mechanical engineers handle all installations professionally. We ensure proper setup for optimal performance and safety.
